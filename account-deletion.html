Account Deletion Request

Dear Valued User,

To initiate the account deletion process, please send an email to contact@holecom.com with the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Username (if applicable)
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

Once we receive your request, our team will:

  1. Verify your account ownership
  2. Process your deletion request
  3. Send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

Please Note: This process may take up to 30 days to complete. During this time, we will verify your identity to ensure the security of your account.

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please include them in your email.

Thank you for your understanding.

Best regards,
The HoleCom Team
